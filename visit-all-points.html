Given a list of points in [x|y] format, find the shortest time required to visit all points. The points will be visited in order and it is assumed to take 1 second to travel one unit horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Points should be a list of coordinates in [x|y] format separated by commas. Clicking Find Shortest Time will return the number of seconds to travel between the points.
