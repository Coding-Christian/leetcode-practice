A self dividing number is a number which is evenly divisible by each of its digits. For example 128 is divisible by 1 (128/1 = 128), 2 (128/2 = 64), and 8 (128/8 = 16). The two inputs under Range should be integers defining the minimum and maxiumum numbers of the desired search range. Clicking Find Self Dividing will return a list of all the self dividing numbers within the range (inclusive). Non-integer numbers will be rounded down.
