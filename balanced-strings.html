Balanced strings are those who have equal quantity of L and R characters. String should be a sequence of up to 100 characters, each either L or R. Any characters other than capital L and R will be ignored. Clicking Find Balanced Substrings will return the number of non-overlapping, balanced substrings that can be made from the balanced string. If the string is not balanced, 0 will be returned.
